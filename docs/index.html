    נסכא דרבי אבא

    Nesikha d'Rabbi Abba

    A witness saw the seizure. The defendant admits the grab but claims the silver was his. A three-way legal bind — and Rabbi Abba's resolution.

    Bava Batra 33b

    שנים אוחזין בטלית

    Shnayim Ochzin

    Two people grabbing a tallis, each claiming it all. The opening Mishnah of Bava Metzia, and the framework for all disputes that follow.

    Bava Metzia 2a

    מאימתי קורין את שמע

    Me'eimatai Korin

    From when may one recite the evening Shema? The Mishnah that opens the entire Shas — and still gets argued about.

    Berakhot 2a
